📖 What is the YAML Tool?

The YAML Tool is a work-in-progress, hands-on tutorial for building your very first character setup YAML. It walks you through each part of a basic setup — character info, gear, weapons, spells and abilities, hunting, crafting, and your training list — one section at a time, in an order that makes sense.

This is designed to be a learning tool, not intended to become a quick generator. The goal is not to click through as fast as possible and walk away with a file — it's to understand what you're building. Every section includes hints on its fields, and every setting you enter appears instantly in a 📋 YAML Preview showing exactly how it will be written into your file, along with a description of what that setting actually does in game.

Please take the time and effort to read the information provided as you go — the hints, the previews, and the descriptions are the tutorial. If something is still unclear after reading, questions are welcome on Discord: kirinartistry.
